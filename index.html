Anti Sleep Alarm For Drivers

A life Saving Project For Drivers

The Antisleep Alarm for Drivers project aims to enhance road safety by preventing accidents caused by drowsy driving. It utilizes wearable or in-car devices with sensors to detect signs of driver fatigue, triggering an alarm (auditory or vibratory) to alert and re-awaken the driver. While effective, the project also emphasizes the importance of drivers prioritizing sufficient rest and taking breaks on long journeys.
